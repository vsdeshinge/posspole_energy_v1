Load Management

Load management refers to the process of balancing the supply and demand of electricity by controlling the consumption of electrical loads. It helps in optimizing energy use and improving the efficiency of power systems.

  • Optimized Consumption: Ensures that energy is used efficiently across systems.
  • Cost Savings: Reduces peak demand charges and optimizes electricity use.
  • Energy Resilience: Enhances grid stability and reliability by managing fluctuations in demand.
